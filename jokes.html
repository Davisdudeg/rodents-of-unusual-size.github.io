Changing out printer cartridges the other day, it occurred to me that magenta and yellow offer no inspiration as subject matter for a song.

But you could write a little ditty about black and cyan.

How do you catch a unique bird?

Unique up on it.

Darth Vader: I know what you are getting for Christmas.

Luke: That's impossible. How could you know that?

Darth Vader: I have felt your presents.

How do you get an elephant up a tree?

Plant an acorn. Have the elephant stand where you planted it. Wait 50 years.

How do you drown a hipster?

Throw him in the mainstream.
